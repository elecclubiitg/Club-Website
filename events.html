Events

These are the events that our club conducts every year which covers all the domains which are there to explore in the field of electronics.

ELECTRIQ

ELECTRIQ is a workshop series where we'll be delving into a variety of subjects, such as the beta values of transistors in analogue electronics, the Ones and Zeroes of digital electronics, EDA Tools used in PCB Design, Raspberry Pi Coding, and much more!

Analog Workshop

Analog workshop is a type of hands-on learning experience that focuses on the design, construction, and operation of analog electronic circuits.The session will cover a variety of fascinating subjects, including the contrast between BJT and MOSFET, the operation and uses of OP AMPS, and much more. Know Your Laptop

The Know Your Laptop workshop is designed to empower individuals to become more proficient and confident in their use of laptops. Whether you're new to laptops or looking to improve your existing skills, this workshop is the perfect opportunity to learn more about the technology you rely on every day.

Digital Workshop

This workshop aims to teach the students about the basics of electronics and how to work with electronic components. The workshop participants will learn to identify and understand the different types of electronic components and how they function in a circuit, how to use basic electronic tools such as multimeter and oscilloscope, and how to design, build, and test simple electronic circuits.

Raspberry Pi Workshop

Raspberry Pi workshop is a training session that teaches students how to use Raspberry Pi, a small, low-cost computer that can be used for a variety of projects and applications. The workshop typically covers the basics of setting up and configuring the Raspberry Pi, as well as an introduction to programming using different software and hardware.

Verilog Workshop

A Verilog workshop is a training program designed to introduce the students to the basics of the Verilog Hardware Description Language (HDL). Verilog is a widely used language for designing and describing digital circuits, and this workshop would cover the core concepts and syntax of the language. The workshop is suitable for engineers, students, and others interested in learning the basics of digital design and Verilog.

AtMegaman Workshop

This workshop aims to provide students with an introduction to the Arduino platform and sensors.The workshop will be interactive and focus on hands-on learning, providing the participants with a solid foundation in the basics of Arduino and sensor usage, and to be able to create their own projects by the end of the workshop.

PCB Design Workshop

This workshop conducted by an electronics club typically involves training participants on the principles and techniques of designing printed circuit boards (PCBs) for electronic devices. The workshop covers topics such as layout and routing, component selection and placement, and testing and verification of the PCB. Hands-on exercises and design projects will also be included to give participants practical experience in designing and fabricating PCBs.
