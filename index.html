What do we do?
Conduct Lectures & Workshops ▾
We offer lectures and workshops on a wide range of topics in electronics, covering everything from selecting a laptop to PCB design and more.
Motivate & Guide Students ▾
We provide guidance and inspiration to students by providing them with the necessary tools and resources to excel in their field of interest.
Provide Resources & Support ▾
We furnish students with the necessary resources and assistance to complete projects from the beginning stages.
About Us
The mission of this club is to divulge students, the challenges and new technologies in the field of electronics and to nurture and amplify their skill set to excel in this arena.

We believe in developing initiatives that are creative and capable of addressing issues that people face in everyday life. To help students understand their projects, we run a variety of lectures, workshops, and mentorship programmes.
